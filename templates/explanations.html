The purpose

The purpose of this app is to provide title hints based on a reference title.

The mechanics about this recommendation come of an algorithm of machine learn called

TfidfVectorizer. Briefly, it compares some texts from each title and creates a matrix

with a coefficient of similarity between each title and all other titles. In this case,

the title text consists of the description, parental guideline, and genre of each title.

Limitations

Sadly, the free acount of heroku cloud platform is very limited.

For this reason, the titles database is limited to titles with an average score greater than 7.
